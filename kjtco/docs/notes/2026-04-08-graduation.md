# kjtcom Graduation Note

**Date:** April 08, 2026
**Iteration:** 10.69.1
**Significance:** Transition to steady-state

Today marks the graduation of kjtcom from an active development lab for the IAO methodology to a steady-state production reference. 

The harness developed here has been externalized to `iao` v0.1.0 and established as its own project. kjtcom remains as the first and most complete proof of the methodology, hosting 6,785 entities across four pipelines.

Future work on kjtcom will focus on data maintenance and schema alignment rather than platform feature development.

The torch passes to `iao` 0.2.0.
